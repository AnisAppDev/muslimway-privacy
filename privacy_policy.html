1. Introduction

Welcome to MuslimWay ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and protection when you use our Islamic companion mobile application.

By downloading, installing, or using MuslimWay, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use the app.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location Data (Optional but Important)

📍 What we access: Your device's approximate and precise location

🎯 Purpose:
  • Calculate accurate prayer times based on your geographical coordinates
  • Determine Qibla (Kaaba) direction from your current position
  • Find nearby mosques and Islamic centers (if enabled)

🔒 How we handle it:
  • Location data is processed locally on your device
  • We do NOT store your location on our servers
  • Location is NOT shared with any third parties
  • You can disable location access anytime in device settings

2.2 App Usage Analytics (Anonymous)

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance and user experience.

Data Type Purpose Identifiable?
App crashes Fix bugs and improve stability No
Feature usage Understand popular features No
Performance metrics Optimize app speed No
Device type Ensure compatibility No

2.3 Local App Data (Stored on Your Device Only)

📖 Reading Progress Last read Quran surah and ayah
🙏 Prayer Preferences Notification settings, calculation method
📿 Tasbih Counts Your dhikr history and daily counts
⭐ Favorites Bookmarked verses, hadiths, and duas
⚙️ App Settings Language, theme, and display preferences
🔔 Reminders Custom prayer and notification alarms
🔐 Important: All local data stays on your device. We cannot access it, and it is not synced to any cloud service unless you explicitly enable backup through your device's built-in backup system (iCloud/Google Backup).

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ Personal Identification: Name, email, phone number, address
  • ❌ Contact Information: We do not access your contacts
  • ❌ Sensitive Data: Religious beliefs, ethnicity, personal beliefs
  • ❌ Financial Information: No in-app purchases, no payment data
  • ❌ Browsing History: We don't track your online activities
  • ❌ Photos/Media: No access to your gallery or camera
  • ❌ Microphone: No audio recording features

4. How We Use Your Information

4.1 Primary Purposes

  1. Prayer Time Calculation: Using your location to provide accurate Islamic prayer times
  2. Qibla Direction: Compass functionality to find the direction of Kaaba
  3. App Improvement: Anonymous crash reports to fix bugs and enhance features
  4. Personalization: Saving your preferences locally for better user experience

4.2 Firebase Services

We use the following Google Firebase services:

  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Error reporting for app stability
  • Firebase Performance: App performance monitoring

These services collect aggregated, anonymous data only and do not identify individual users.

5.1 Service Providers

We only use trusted service providers for essential app functionality:

  • Google Firebase: Analytics and crash reporting (anonymous data only)
  • Google Play Services: App distribution and updates

5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information only if required by:

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Location Permissions

You have full control over location access:

  • Allow all the time: For accurate prayer time notifications
  • Allow only while using the app: Manual prayer time checks
  • Deny: You can still use the app by manually setting your location

To change: Go to Settings → Apps → MuslimWay → Permissions → Location

7.2 Notification Preferences

Control which notifications you receive:

  • Prayer time alerts
  • Daily hadith reminders
  • Friday sermon times
  • Islamic event notifications

7.3 Data Access and Deletion

Your Rights:
  • ✅ Access: All your data is visible within the app
  • ✅ Export: Use device backup to save your data
  • ✅ Delete: Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • ✅ Opt-out: Disable analytics in app settings
